SSA Your PDGA Player Rating (PR) is a number that shows how close your average round scores are compared to the course rating, called the SSA (Scratch Scoring Averages, of the courses you’ve played in competition. Players who average the SSA on courses played will have a rating of 1000 and are considered “scratch players”. A player who averages scores lower than SSAs on course they’ve played will have a rating over 1000.
